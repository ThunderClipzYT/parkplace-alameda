Our Story

In the heart of Alameda's Park Street district.

ParkPlace Alameda sits surrounded by local restaurants, cafés, shops, and small businesses. Our building offers private office suites for people who are building, growing, or expanding their work.

We're home to a mix of professionals: wellness providers, beauty professionals, consultants, therapists, and other small business owners. We know how much it matters to have a space that feels professional, comfortable, and easy for clients to visit.

That's why our suites include the things that matter: WiFi, utilities, common areas, secured access, and a large parking lot in back. ParkPlace is more than office space. It's a place to work, meet clients, and be part of the community.
